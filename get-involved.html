Get Involved

Join us in making health education more equitable, inclusive, and accessible for all. The Equity & Health Literacy Project (EHLP) is powered by dedicated volunteers who bring language skills, lived experience, and passion for community care. Whether you're interested in translation, workshop facilitation, outreach, or content creation—there's a place for you on our team.

We're currently accepting applications for volunteers and collaborators who want to support our mission. Opportunities range from one-time event support to ongoing program roles.

Volunteer Application Form

Please upload your resume in PDF format

Ways to Get Involved:

  • Translation & Content Creation: Help us develop and translate health education materials into different languages. We need writers, editors, and translators who can make complex health information clear and accessible.
  • Workshop Facilitation: Lead or assist with health literacy workshops in your community. Share your expertise and help others understand important health information.
  • Community Outreach: Help us connect with communities and spread awareness about our programs and resources.
  • Research & Evaluation: Contribute to our understanding of health literacy needs and program effectiveness through data collection and analysis.
