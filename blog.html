Blog

Welcome to the EHLP Blog — a space where we share stories, insights, and reflections on health equity, community engagement, and culturally responsive education.

Here, you'll find updates on our work, opinion pieces from our team and collaborators, and highlights from the communities we serve. Whether we're unpacking public health trends, breaking down healthcare policy, or spotlighting lived experiences, our blog is a space for learning, dialogue, and action.

How to Obtain Affordable Health Insurance in Ontario — No Matter Your Status

When it comes to healthcare in Ontario, eye care is one of the most commonly misunderstood areas—particularly around what’s covered, who qualifies, and where to find affordable options. With the rising cost of glasses, exams, and treatments, many Ontarians—especially newcomers, students, and uninsured individuals—are struggling to access basic vision care.
