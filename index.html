Our Mission

The Immigrant Health Literacy Project (IHLP) aims to improve healthcare literacy, accessibility, and equity for immigrant, marginalized, and racialized communities. We will do this through tailored informational pamphlets, workshops, and digital content.

Overview

IHLP is committed to improving healthcare literacy, accessibility, and equity for immigrant, marginalized, and racialized communities. The organization plans to achieve this through tailored informational pamphlets, workshops, and digital content, including social media, newsletters, and blog posts.

Short-term Goals

  • Finalizing two pamphlet designs and piloting distribution
  • Launching a fully functional website with blog posts
  • Establishing consistent social media presence

Long-term Objectives

  • Securing an OTF grant
  • Conducting research
  • Incorporating as a nonprofit
  • Making tangible policy changes
  • Running ongoing workshops and outreach
